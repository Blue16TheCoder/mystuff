Prime Numbers are numbers that can be multipied by only 2 factors and every prime

Composite numbers are numbers that have more than 2 factors and they can be multipied by more ways
and they include 9 since it can be multipied by 1, 9 and 3 so it is a composite number.

Special number is a number that is not prime nor composite because they only have one factor. The
