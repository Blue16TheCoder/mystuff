About images

The thing above is called an image, a visual graphic that tells many things like a picture. It can have different forms like PNG and JPEG To make an image in HTML, you use the tag and inside of it has different attributes. The common ones in img are src which tells the source of the image. The source can be the image file. The alt attribute tells the alternate text if the image gets broken. They are often linked or embedded into webpages.
